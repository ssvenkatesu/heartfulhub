Earthquake

Before

Prepare an emergency kit, secure heavy furniture, and create a family communication plan.

During

Drop, cover, and hold on. Stay indoors until the shaking stops.

After

Check for injuries and damages, and follow emergency protocols.

Flood

Before

Know your flood risk, prepare an emergency kit, and have a plan for evacuation.

During

Move to higher ground, avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.

After

Avoid floodwaters, clean and disinfect everything that got wet, and seek assistance if needed.

Fire

Before

Install smoke alarms, plan and practice a fire escape plan, and keep fire extinguishers handy.

During

Evacuate immediately if you hear the fire alarm, and use stairs not the elevator.

After

Return to the building only when authorities say it is safe, and document any property damage.

Hurricane

Before

Prepare an emergency kit, secure your home, and know your evacuation routes.

During

Stay indoors, away from windows, and follow instructions from local authorities.

After

Stay alert for flooding, avoid downed power lines, and check on your neighbors.
